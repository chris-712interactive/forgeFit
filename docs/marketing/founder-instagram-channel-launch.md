## 2. Why two accounts

| | **@repwithchris** (this channel) | **@forgerep** (brand) |
|--|----------------------------------|------------------------|
| **Job** | Trust, story, proof of life | Product education + signup conversion |
| **Face** | You on camera every day | App UI, demos, carousels |
| **CTA** | Soft: “tools I use in bio” / tag brand | Hard: Start free → signup link |
| **Content feel** | Gym floor, progress, honesty | Polished demos + myth vs fact |
| **Risk if merged** | Brand feed becomes “just a founder diary” and loses product SEO; personal feed becomes an ad and dies | Same |

**Rule of thumb:** Personal account *shows the life*. Brand account *sells the tool*. Cross-tag; never duplicate every post.

## 3. Positioning & audience

### One-line position

> Fort Myers dad / founder documenting an evidence-based training journey — building ForgeRep in public while actually using it in the gym.

### Who follows

- Men 25–45 who lift but hate app hopping
- People burned by bro-science and AI workout generators
- Founders / builders who like “build in public” + fitness
- Locals (SWFL / Fort Myers) for early collabs and gym social proof

### What you are *not*

- Aesthetic influencer with perfect lighting every day
- Generic motivation page
- A second ForgeRep ads account

**Tone:** Direct, warm, slightly nerdy about evidence. Never shame. Never fake “day 1 of my transformation” if you’ve been training for years — lead with *builder + lifter documenting the system*.

## 7. Content pillars & mix

Aim for a **repeatable mix**, not perfection.

| Pillar | % of posts | Examples |
|--------|------------|----------|
| **A — Training truth** | 40% | Working sets, form notes, “what progressive overload looked like this week” |
| **B — Build in public** | 25% | Shipped feature, bug you hit in the gym, offline win |
| **C — Evidence / myth** | 20% | One myth, one citation-level plain-English take (point to `@forgerep` carousels for depth) |
| **D — Life / dad / FL** | 10% | Carline, Fort Myers gym life, schedule reality — human glue |
| **E — Direct product** | 5% | Explicit “I built this” demos — always disclosed |

### Formats that win on Reels (priority order)

1. **Hook face → gym proof → one lesson** (15–25s)
2. **Screen + face split** (app logging in dead zone)
3. **Carousel** “Week in numbers” (saves > likes)
4. **Duets / greenscreen** reacting to fitness myths
5. **GRWM / day-in-the-life** only if paced fast (under 30s)

### Hook starters

Steal and rotate:

- “I built a fitness app — here’s what I still get wrong in the gym.”
- “Stop trusting AI for your program. Here’s what I use instead.”
- “My gym has no signal. Watch this still log.”
- “Week __ of training with my own product.”
- “If you’re over 30 and tired of restarting Mondays…”
- “Bro-science said X. Evidence says Y.”
- “Dad schedule. Full program. No 2-hour workouts.”

### Audio

- Trending audio for reach **or** original VO for authority — alternate
- Keep VO under 1–2 ideas per Reel
- Save 3–5 “brand beats” (non-trending) for product demos so they don’t age out

## 8. Cadence & batching

### Minimum viable cadence (founder with a day job)

| Surface | Cadence |
|---------|---------|
| Reels | **5–7 / week** (daily is ideal first 30 days) |
| Carousel / static | **2–3 / week** |
| Stories | **5–12 / day** on training days (raw, not polished) |
| TikTok | Same Reel file, post within 1 hour of IG |
| Brand account `@forgerep` | Run [31-day calendar](./instagram-31-day-calendar.md) separately — do not skip it because personal is growing |

### Weekly batch rhythm

| Day | Work |
|-----|------|
| **Sun** | Script 7 hooks · pick trending audios · schedule placeholders in Meta Business Suite |
| **Gym sessions** | Film only — no editing in the locker room |
| **Mon / Thu night** | CapCut batch: cut 3–4 Reels each night (~60–90 min) |
| **Daily** | Post 1 Reel · 8–15 Stories · reply to every comment for first 60 days |

**Time budget:** ~45–75 min/day once the clip bank exists. Protect Sundays for batch planning.

## 10. Caption / CTA / disclosure rules

### Caption formula

```
HOOK line (same as on-screen)

1–3 short lines of value

CTA: Follow for the journey · App I built is in bio
Tag: @forgerep when it’s product-relevant
```

### Disclosure (non-negotiable)

Whenever ForgeRep is shown or recommended:

> I built ForgeRep — my own app.

Pin that comment on strong product Reels. Meta / FTC expect clear material connection; founder status is a feature, not something to hide.

### Hashtags (8–12, rotate)

**Journey set:** `#fitnessjourney #gymtok #evidencebasedtraining #mensfitness #over30fitness #progressiveoverload #dadlife #fortmyers`

**Product-adjacent set:** `#fitnessapp #workoutlog #macrotracking #offlinefitness #strengthtraining #buildinpublic`

Do **not** paste 30 tags. Do **not** use the exact same set as `@forgerep` every time — overlap 40%, differentiate 60%.

### Comments & DMs

- Reply with a question (boosts distribution)
- DM anyone who asks “what app?” with the disclosure + link
- Never argue nutrition dogma in comments — invite to a Reel

## 11. Launch week (Days 1–7)

Post even if follower count is zero. The algorithm needs a content graph more than a following.

| Day | Reel | Other | Notes |
|-----|------|-------|-------|
| **1** | “I built a fitness app — here’s why” (origin, 45–60s) | Story: 5-slide intro | Pin later |
| **2** | Gym working set + one cue | Stories from session | Face + lift |
| **3** | Offline logging proof in gym | Carousel: “What I track weekly” | Tag `@forgerep` |
| **4** | Myth vs reality (short) | Point deeper education to brand account | Soft handoff |
| **5** | Build-in-public: one shipped thing | Desk B-roll | Stay human |
| **6** | Dad / schedule training Reel | Poll in Stories: cut vs bulk questions | Engagement |
| **7** | Week 1 recap + “follow if you’re done restarting Mondays” | Soft CTA to bio | Pin if strong |

**Do not** launch with a giveaway on Day 1. Earn 20–30 posts of trust first.

## 12. Growth system (Weeks 2–8)

| Tactic | How |
|--------|-----|
| **Collab posts** | 1×/week with SWFL gym / micro creator; you bring app proof, they bring audience |
| **Seeding** | Share Reels to 3–5 Stories of friends; post in relevant Facebook Reels too |
| **Brand handshake** | `@forgerep` Stories reshare your best personal Reels 2×/week; personal tags brand on product posts |
| **Series** | Name one series and repeat (“Forge Fridays” = week stats + one lesson) |
| **TikTok** | Native captions; don’t watermark with IG |
| **Consistency > virality** | One 50k view Reel means nothing without daily follow-up |

### What to stop doing if growth stalls

- Posting only product demos
- Posting only faceless B-roll
- Buying followers
- Changing handle mid-growth

## 13. Metrics that matter

Track weekly (Sunday night, 10 minutes):

| Metric | Early target |
|--------|--------------|
| Reels posted | ≥5 |
| Avg Reel views | Climbing week over week |
| Profile visits | Spike after best Reel |
| Follows from Reels | Watch ratio in Insights |
| Bio link clicks | UTM `founder_bio` |
| Signups attributed | Even 3–5/week = working |
| Saves / shares | On education + progress posts |

**Ignore:** Vanity likes from non-ICP accounts.  
**Optimize for:** Saves, shares, profile visits, and signup UTM — not follower count alone.
