## 2. Attribution

- First durable touch wins (link or code). Later affiliate clicks do not overwrite.
- Partner contact email and portal users cannot earn on their own accounts (self-referral ban).
- Comp / free seats do not generate commission.

---

## 3. Reporting

- Company provides monthly CSV (or portal access) with: attributed signups, paid conversions, gross, commission.
- Aggregate metrics only unless a separate DPA/consent requires PII.
- Gym optional: club-level breakdown via `?club=` on tracked links.
